Before
I chose this location because I found it interesting that there is a paved sidewalk on a slope where almost no one walks. In fact, I conducted a survey of 17 people, and none of them were aware that the sidewalk even existed. This led me to consider ideas such as installing a large poster on the slope or spraying artificial snow to transform it into something resembling a sledding hill. However, I wanted to attract people's attention with a simpler intervention than a poster, and the artificial snow idea had the drawback of being difficult to maintain due to weather conditions.
